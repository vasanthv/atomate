Privacy policy

We do not collect any user data. We do not have user sign up or login in our application, so we do not have any way to collect user data.

We limit ourselves to storing the smallest amount of data that we need to create a seamless experience. This data includes:

  • RSS or Atom feeds you added to our website (we call it Channels)
  • we fetch and save the Channels in a frequent intervals

We also use Cabin Analytics to track aggregated usage statistics to improve our service.

The makers of Atomate have no intention of using personally or selling any of the above-mentioned data.
